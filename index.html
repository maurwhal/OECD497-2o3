Assay calls (per study)

2o3 Decision (hazard)

Final: Inconclusive
    Rule: Two concordant, High‑confidence results across ≥2 KEs → conclusive prediction. Otherwise, Inconclusive. BR results and any assay flagged as limited are treated as Low confidence for the 2o3 combination.
    Note: kDPRA is recorded for potency/IATA context and is not used in the 2o3 hazard aggregation.

    Borderline Ranges (BR) Cheat‑Sheet

    BR results are treated as Low confidence in the 2‑out‑of‑3 combination.
    KE1 – Protein binding
    • DPRA (mean): Positive ≥ 6.38%; BR 4.95–8.32
    • DPRA (Cys‑only): Positive ≥ 13.89%; BR 10.56–18.47
    • ADRA (mean): Positive ≥ 4.9%; BR 4.1–5.9
    • ADRA (NAC‑only): Positive ≥ 5.6%; BR 4.7–6.8
    • kDPRA: Potency context; not used in 2o3 hazard.
    KE2 – Keratinocyte activation
    • KeratinoSens: Positive ≥ 1.5× (viability ≥70%); BR 1.35–1.67
    • LuSens: Positive ≥ 1.5×; BR 1.28–1.76
    • EpiSensA: Positive if ≥2 genes exceed thresholds (ATF3>15, GCLM>2, DNAJB2>2, IL‑8>4); BRs ATF3 10.71–21.02; GCLM 1.64–2.45; DNAJB2 1.61–2.52; IL‑8 3.11–5.16.
    KE3 – Dendritic cell activation
    • h‑CLAT: CD86 ≥150% and/or CD54 ≥200%; BRs CD86 122–184%; CD54 157–255%.
    • U‑SENS: SI(CD86) ≥150%; BR 128–176%
    • IL‑8 Luc: Positive ≥ 1.4×; BR 1.25–1.57
    • GARDskin: Positive if mean DV > 0; BR −0.450 to +0.450
    BR and any flagged limitation → Low confidence for that method. The 2o3 rule needs two concordant High‑confidence results across different KEs.
